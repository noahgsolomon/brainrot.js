## What Even Is "Brainrot"?

"Brainrot" is a term for the hyper-stimulating, chaotic, absurdly entertaining short-form videos that dominate TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and Instagram Reels. Think: two AI characters debating whether water is wet while Subway Surfers gameplay plays in the background.

It sounds like it shouldn't work. It works incredibly well.

## The Anatomy of a Brainrot Video

Most brainrot videos share a few key ingredients:

1. **AI-generated voices** — Celebrity or character voices saying things they'd never actually say
2. **Split-screen gameplay** — Subway Surfers, Minecraft parkour, or satisfying slime videos in the background
3. **Rapid-fire subtitles** — Bold, colorful text that keeps your eyes locked on the screen
4. **Absurd premises** — The more ridiculous the topic, the better it performs

## Why Does Brainrot Content Go Viral?

The psychology is actually fascinating:

- **Dual stimulation** — The gameplay + dialogue combo engages multiple attention channels simultaneously
- **Low commitment** — Videos are 30-90 seconds, so there's no reason not to watch
- **Shareability** — "You HAVE to see this" factor is off the charts
- **Algorithm-friendly** — High watch time + shares = algorithmic boost
